Signal Fire Party Support

Local Multiplayer Party Game  ·  Version 1.0

Thanks for playing Signal Fire Party. If you are running into an issue with hosting, joining, or keeping a same-room session connected, this page covers the basics.

Nearby players are not appearing

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on both devices and that each device has granted the permissions requested by the app. Keep the devices nearby, return to the lobby list, and wait a few seconds for the host session to appear.

I denied a permission prompt by mistake

Open the iPhone Settings app, find Signal Fire Party, and re-enable the permissions requested for nearby multiplayer discovery and lobby visibility.

The session stopped or someone dropped from the game

Move the devices closer together, reopen the app if needed, and try rejoining the hosted session from the lobby list. Short interruptions can happen if devices move out of nearby Bluetooth range or if the host leaves the session.

The app is not opening or is behaving unexpectedly

Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app. Because gameplay data is stored locally, reinstalling the app will reset local app data.
